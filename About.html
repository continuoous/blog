HERE'S WHAT MY DAY LOOKS LIKE AT WORK:

I mostly spend time sorting an evergrowing list of business needs to optimize IT resources to keep projects advancing at fast pace.

So proud that I didn't mention "Backlog" or "funnel". But, had to throw in "optimize" to sound savvy.



Expertise of Business Process and IT Solution is how I prioritize business requirements and simplify them adequately so their IT solutions become feasible.

Negotiation and influence is, everyday, par for the course and is mostly why I still have a job.



My days are exhilarating for the need to get up to speed quickly on a new business issues and articulate why calculated risks are called for to avoid stalling on progress.

Seriously though, clear, unambiguous choices are a rarity. Decision making under uncertainty is my art



DAY OUTSIDE WORK:

In the context of my work, I stay up to date on a variety of topics about technology, business trends and decision science.
